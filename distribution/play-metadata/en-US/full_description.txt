Flashlight is a fast, simple torch app for Android. Open it when you need light right now, or add the Quick Settings tile and home-screen widget for one-tap access.

Core features:

- Instant LED torch with a large, clear toggle
- Quick Settings tile for faster access from Android system controls
- Home-screen widget to open or toggle the torch
- Screen light mode with brightness and warm, neutral, cool, and night-red colors
- Steady, strobe, and SOS torch modes
- Shake-to-toggle option
- Auto-off timer
- Haptic and sound feedback options

Built for practical use:

- Clean interface that stays readable in the dark
- Camera permission is used only to control the LED torch
- No account required
- Privacy policy included in the app and store listing

Flashlight includes ads to keep the app free. Ads are placed away from the main torch button so the light stays quick to use.
